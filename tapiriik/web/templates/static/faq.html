Questions: Answered

What do I have to do to use tapiriik?

Just sign in to all the services you would like to sync between, click "Synchronize now," then forget ever reading this sentence.

Do I need to re-connect my accounts every time?

No! Just connect to any one of the services you have already connected, and you will be signed back into tapiriik and ready to start another synchronization.

Do I need to visit tapiriik.com to sync activities?

For $2/year, synchronization is fully automatic. If you can't manage that, you can visit tapiriik.com as often as you want to manually trigger a synchronization.

$2?! I should really call my accountant first…

Synchronizing activities once costs almost nothing. Doing it 24 times a day, 365 days a year: less so. You paying $2 a year covers these costs so tapiriik will always run smoothly.

Which activities get synchronized?

All supported activities in your account are synchronized, not just new ones. Manually entered activities are supported, but are not currently being synchronized to TrainingPeaks (still some bugs to work out there).

Will tapiriik create duplicate activities?

Nope - even if you tracked the same activity on multiple devices, or with multiple apps, tapiriik will see that and won't create any duplicate activities.

What about activities I've marked as private?

By default, tapiriik will sync private activities. If this is not the behaviour you desire, you can opt to sync only public activities from certain services. Remember that not all services support private activities, and you should be cognizant of your privacy settings across all of your accounts.

What data gets synchronized?

As much as possible: in addition to your path, tapiriik will also sync distance, speed, heart rate, cadence, power, calories burned and even temperature between all services which support them.

The activity statistics changed after a sync!

Discrepencies in activity statistics, like elevation gained/lost or calories burned, appear when services use different algorithms to calculate the same values. tapiriik does not calculate these during synchronization.

Can I sync in only one direction?

Yes: before triggering a sync, open up the options for the service in question and tweak as desired. These preferences are applied to all activities - even after they've been synchronized to other services.

What are "untagged activites" and why do I care?

GPX and TCX files in your Dropbox don't include information about the type of activity they contain. You need to include the type of the activity in the file name, or place the file in an appropriately named folder.

Some activities could not be synchronized?!

 Sometimes there are activities that tapiriik has trouble synchronizing. Most of the time this is a result of a temporary error communicating with the remote site. If the issue persists, send me an email.

How do I connect more services?

Just sign into any one of the services you already connected, then sign into the new service (or the other way around, it doesn't matter). The new service will be synchronized with all the others.

Does tapiriik ever modify or delete my activities?

No. Simple as that.

What data is stored on your servers?

As little as is required. Check out the privacy page for more.

When will tapiriik support Activity Tracking Site X?

You can register your interest in a particular site at the supported services poll. This helps prioritize development, but I can't make promises - especially about release timelines.

Can tapiriik and CopyMySports be used together?

I wouldn't recommend it - CopyMySports will often create duplicates of activities tapiriik has already synchronized.

tapiriik stores...

  • Details of your activities (path, time, etc...)
  • The authentication tokens used to read and upload activities
  • Your email - but only if you opt into having it stored for diagnostic purposes

...until you disconnect your account or they are no longer needed, whichever is sooner

What about my email and password?

In most cases, tapiriik never stores (or even knows) your email or password.

The only exception to this is if you connect your Garmin Connect account: there's no way for tapiriik to access your account without your username and password. In this case, and only this case, your credentials will be stored in an encrypted‡ form on the tapiriik server. If you opt to have your Garmin Connect credentials remembered, they will be retained until you disconnect your account. If you do not opt to have them remembered — the default — they will be erased after your account's next synchronization.

‡ I've done a good deal to keep your Garmin Connect credentials safe, but "encrypted" does not mean "100% secure." There has to be a way for tapiriik to decrypt them to sync your account, and therefore anyone with enough access and resources could decrypt them, too.

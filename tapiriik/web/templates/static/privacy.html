tapiriik stores...

  • Details of your activities (path, time, etc...)
  • The authentication tokens used to read and upload activities
  • Your email - but only if you opt into having it stored for diagnostic purposes

...until you disconnect your account or they are no longer needed, whichever is sooner

What about my email and password?

In most cases, tapiriik never stores (or even knows) your email or password.

Garmin Connect and SportTracks.mobi credentials are stored‡ only if you opt in.

‡ I've done a good deal to keep these credentials safe, but there has to be a way for tapiriik to use them to sync your account. Therefore, anyone with enough access and resources to the tapiriik database and servers could use them, too.
